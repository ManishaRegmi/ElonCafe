Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the cafe located?

Located at Maitidevi, Kathmandu.Our address is 1415 Third Avenue Seattle WA 98101. We’re in downtown Seattle on Third Avenue between Union and Pike. For a map and directions to the cafe.


What are your cafe hours?

Our cafe is open Monday to Friday 11am to 2pm. The earliest pickup time for your online order is 11am, latest is 2pm. We’re closed on Federal Holidays and bank holidays.


What’s the difference between the catering and cafe menus?

Our catering menu is designed for upcoming meetings and events and includes delivery to your meeting location. Our cafe menu is designed for same-day, to-go orders and is available for priority pickup from our cafe.


How soon in advance should I place my cafe pickup order?

It depends on the size of your order. Your order can be ready in as little as 15 minutes if it is for one or two items. If you order more than that, it may take a bit longer because everything is made fresh to order. When you checkout you will be asked to choose a pickup time and your earliest pickup time will be calculated on the size of your order and the time it is being placed.


What form of payment do you accept

We accept Visa, Mastercard and American Express credit cards. For Catering orders we also accept company checks and we offer house accounts for frequent customers that preferred to be billed monthly.


Do you do special requests?

Yes. There is a special requests field on all our menu items online so that you can tag any item with a special request when you add it to your cart. If you have a request that cannot be entered this way please give us a call at 206.264.7900 and we’ll make sure these requests are taken care of.
